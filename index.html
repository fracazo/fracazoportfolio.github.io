Experience

Before that, I spent several years leading and delivering product design work for companies like Qantas, Vodafone, Woolworths, and NAB, designing web and mobile experiences used by millions of people.

How I work

I'm most effective when working on problems that are:

  • unclear or under-defined
  • shared across multiple teams
  • constrained by legacy systems or organisational complexity

I prefer written, async collaboration for day-to-day work, and use synchronous conversations when alignment, ambiguity, or feedback quality really matters.
